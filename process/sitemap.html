Corvid Research


Intended audience

The intended audience for this website are those with specific questions or just a general interest in corvids. This website is helpful for those who wish to read soley about corvids.


Main purpose

The main purpose of this website is to display the dedicated research of corvids by a woman named Kaeli Swift. She mentions that ever since she was a child, she loved wildlife, especially birds, and discovered that crows and other corvids offer answers about animal behavior and cognition that people, and herself, have been questioning for years.
