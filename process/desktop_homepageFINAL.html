“Where land meets the table”

Pines of Nowhere is a Modern American restaurant dedicated to seasonal cooking and sourcing from local farms and waters. Our menu showcases the bounty of the Chesapeake Bay watershed in thoughtful, delicious and beautiful dishes that aim to nourish and surprise. Every plate we serve is a reflection of our deep love and respect for our region’s natural rhythms and the hardworking farmers, fishers, and artisans who help bring it to life. With a focus on freshness, creativity, and hospitality, Pines of Nowhere invites guests to slow down, savor the seasons, and experience the beauty of local ingredients prepared with care and intention. More than just a place to dine, Pines of Nowhere is a space for connection — between people, place, and the food that sustains us. Our vision is to create a warm, welcoming environment where guests feel rooted in the landscape that surrounds them, whether through a simple heirloom tomato salad or a wood-fired catch of the day. We believe in cooking that honors both tradition and innovation, celebrating the past while imagining new ways to express the spirit of the Mid-Atlantic. Each visit is an invitation to gather, share stories, and taste the rhythm of the seasons, one plate at a time.

  1. Harvest Mondays: Enjoy 3 courses for $35, featuring chef-selected dishes that showcase the week's freshest ingredients. Ease into the week with three seasonal courses celebrating the best from our local farms and waters. Thoughtful, comforting, and full of flavor, Harvest Mondays are a delicious way to taste the season.
  2. Tide to Table Tuesdays: $1 oysters + Chesapeake seafood specials all night long. Each Tuesday, we celebrate the Bay and all it gives. Enjoy freshly shucked local oysters for $1 each, alongside a selection of chef-inspired seafood dishes that change with the tides. From delicate rockfish and blue crab to sweet shrimp and seasonal catches, every plate reflects the freshness and spirit of the Chesapeake Bay.
  3. Wine Down Wednesdays: Half-priced bottles from our curated regional wine list. Enjoy half-priced bottles from our thoughtfully curated wine list, featuring standout producers from the Mid-Atlantic and beyond. We invite you to slow down, sip, and savor the beauty of good wine and good company.
  4. Sunday Supper: A comforting, family-style meal for $28 per person that celebrates the season’s freshest ingredients. Served in generous portions to share, each Sunday Supper features thoughtfully prepared dishes — from wood-roasted meats and hearty grains to vibrant vegetables and seasonal sides. Come hungry, leave happy.
